About Recto

Recto Memorial National High School is located at Brgy. Quipot, Tiaong, Quezon. Formerly known as the Tayabas Academy, it was a private school established in 1941 during the mayoralty of Mr. Petronio Pasumbal. It flourished for a number of years and started to provide excellent services to the neighboring towns. The Luzonian Institute was renamed in 1964, and while it was at the pinnacle of its growth and success, the management made the decision to broaden its offerings in response to a rise in enrollment and the town's perceived need for more educational options. In honor of Don Claro M. Recto, who was rumored to have been born in Tiaong, the management renamed the Luzonian Institute as Recto Memorial College on February 8, 1965, adding a tertiary level.
